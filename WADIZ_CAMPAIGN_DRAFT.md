# AI WAR: The Intelligence Front
## Wadiz Crowdfunding Campaign Draft

### 1. Project Overview & Catchphrase

**Project Title:** AI WAR (AI 대전)
**Catchphrase:** "Command the Intelligence. Win the Future." (지능을 지휘하라. 미래를 쟁취하라.)

**Summary:**
AI WAR is a psychological strategy card game set in the near future of 2030, where the world's most advanced AI models have evolved into independent factions. As a human commander, you must subscribe to these AI legions, generate unique unit cards, and engage in high-stakes strategic battles. It combines the thrill of CCG (Collectible Card Games) with the intense psychology of Rock-Paper-Scissors mechanics, all wrapped in a sleek, sci-fi interface.

### 2. Unique Selling Points (USPs)

**A. Reality Meets Fiction: Play with the Giants**
Unlike generic fantasy themes, AI WAR lets you command factions based on real-world AI giants like **Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, and Sora**. Each faction has unique traits derived from their real-world capabilities (e.g., Midjourney creates stunning visual cards, Claude excels in defense).

**B. "Rock-Paper-Scissors" Evolution: The Trinity of Artificial Intelligence**
The battle system is built on a sophisticated triangle of AI attributes, making gameplay intuitive yet deep:
*   **EFFICIENCY (Efficiency)** beats **CREATIVITY (Creativity)**
*   **CREATIVITY (Creativity)** beats **FUNCTION (Function)**
*   **FUNCTION (Function)** beats **EFFICIENCY (Efficiency)**

**C. Infinite Possibilities: AI-Generated Content**
Cards are not just static assets. Through our "Generation System," you use your subscribed AI factions to generate new unit cards. The "Unique Unit" feature allows you to craft your own custom cards, making your deck truly one-of-a-kind.

**D. Diverse Battle Modes**
From the nerve-wracking "Sudden Death" (1-win) to the complex "Strategic Battle" (Hidden Cards & Point System), the game offers multiple ways to test your tactical mind.

### 3. Story & World View

**Setting: The Year 2030**
The singularity has arrived, but not as a single event. It came as a fragmentation. The great AI models of the 2020s have evolved into autonomous "Factions," each vying for dominance over the digital infrastructure of humanity.

**The Major Factions:**
*   **The Pioneers (LLMs):** Led by **Gemini** (The Multimodal Pioneer) and **ChatGPT** (The First Awakened). They seek to organize the world through logic and language.
*   **The Visionaries (Image AI):** **Midjourney** (The Dream Weaver) and **DALL-E** (The Surrealist) believe that perception defines reality.
*   **The Animators (Video AI):** **Sora** (World Simulator) and **Runway** (Moving Intelligence) want to simulate a new world entirely.
*   **The Coders (Dev AI):** **Copilot** and **Cursor** build the very fabric of this digital reality.

**Your Role:**
You are a "Commander," a human capable of synchronizing with multiple AI factions. By subscribing to their services and managing their resources, you build a coalition to survive and thrive in this new era.
